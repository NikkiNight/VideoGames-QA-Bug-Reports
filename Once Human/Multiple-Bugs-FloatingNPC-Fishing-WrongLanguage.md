# Multiple Bugs Reported

## Bug Report A - Floating NPC

- The NPC, Brad Lito, in Meyer's Market is floating rather than standing on ground.

<br />
- Platform: PC/Mobile. <br />
- Affected Feature: None, immersion. <br />
- Scenario: Manibus.

## Evidence
<img src="Media/Floating-NPC-Brad-Lito.jpg" alt="Image of NPC Brad Lito floating" width="300">

## Why is this issue important?
- From a player standpoint, it affects immersion of the game and displays a lack of professional polish.
- From a QA standpoint, breaks immersion. While not a gameplay blocker, it creates a noticeable visual inconsistency that reduces overall polish of the world environment.
